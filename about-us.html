ABOUT US
Charity is an organization that helps those in need mainly by raising money through raising donations. Donations may also come in form of food,clothing and other goods, education etc. Furthermore, it can be a way to give back to society by offering assistance as volunteers. Its main motive is to help others and not to make any profit out of it.Children are the future of our society. So, it is imperative for us as a society to take care of them and provide them with all the necessary resources. In order to do so, we have various charity organizations that work towards this goal. One such organization is Child Aid International which provides relief and support to children living in poverty in developing countries with a focus on education and health care. Child Aid International works with communities and governments to ensure that children have access to quality education. They also provide health care services like vaccines, medical treatment, nutrition services etc., which address the needs of these children. Child Aid International has been helping children since 1979 by providing reliefs and support for them in developing countries like India, Bangladesh, Cambodia etc.,
